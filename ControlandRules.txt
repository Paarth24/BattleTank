CONTROLS -
Player 1 -
W - Move forward
A - Move left
S - Move Down
D - Move right
F - Shoot Bullet

Player 2 -
Arrow key up - Move forward
Arrow key left - Move left
Arrow key down - Move Down
Arrow key right - Move right
M - Shoot Bullet

Escape - Jump back to main menu


RULES - 
3 basic tanks will spawn in the beginning of each level.
Other tanks will spawn randomly in a time period between 5 seconds - 60 seconds.
You can't restart a level, if the level is lost u have to jump back to main menu.
